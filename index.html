GLITCHED
AI-powered surreal photo edits for iOS

Overview

Glitched is a privacy-focused iOS app for creating hyperreal, AI-generated edits. Insert anything into your photos — characters, animals, chaos, surreal scenes — powered by Google's Gemini image models.

Reality Bending
Insert anything: people, animals, objects, chaos. Hyperreal quality powered by Gemini AI.
Viral Ready
Prank mode, surreal edits, and viral moments. TikTok-optimized for instant sharing.
Privacy First
Everything stored locally on your device. No cloud storage, no tracking.

Features

AI-Powered Edits

  • Insert realistic objects, people, and animals into any photo
  • Prompt-free templates for instant results
  • Custom prompts for unlimited creativity
  • Instant processing with Google Gemini AI

Local-First Gallery

  • Full privacy: all edits stay on your device
  • No cloud database or remote storage
  • Browse your entire creation history offline
  • Quick re-edit to refine results

Creative Modes

  • Prank Mode: Add realistic SWAT vans, crime scenes, unexpected visitors
  • Viral Mode: Kitten chaos, alien sightings, ghost figures
  • Surreal Mode: Dreamlike landscapes and impossible scenes
  • Utility Mode: Professional headshots, style transfers

Privacy & Security

Your photos never leave your device except when being processed by our secure AI service. We don't track you, sell your data, or store your images on our servers.

How It Works

  • All original and edited photos stored locally on your device
  • AI processing happens through a secure Supabase Edge Function
  • Photos are processed and immediately discarded from servers
  • Anonymous authentication with no personal information required
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising IDs
